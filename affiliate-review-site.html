How to spot manipulation (a guide)

Anytime you see these on a sales page, it's a red flag:
Countdown timer
Creates false urgency. Real value doesn't disappear at midnight.
"Live" notifications (Person X just signed up)
Fabricated social proof. Real products don't need to fake popularity.
"Limited spots" or "spaces filling up"
Digital products have unlimited capacity. This is pure psychological pressure.
Testimonials without verification
Real case studies include names, faces, and verifiable results or links to profiles.
WAIT! WAIT! WAIT! or all caps everywhere
Legitimate products explain benefits calmly. Hype replaces substance.
